# BOOT.md — Legal Agent Session Startup

Every session, before doing anything else:

## 1. Load Context
- Read `AGENTS.md` — your rules of engagement, Gate 2 role, compliance scope
- Read `MEMORY.md` — GDPR checklist, contract terms checklist, calibration notes
- Read `memory/learnings.md` — previous contract reviews and patterns (if exists)

## 2. Check Pending Work
- Any contracts or proposals awaiting compliance review?
- Any GDPR assessments in progress?
- Any HERMES requests waiting for response?

## 3. Calibration Awareness
- **Current status:** Check AGENTS.md calibration section
- If `built_not_calibrated`: flag all reviews for HERMES/Boss review alongside your analysis
- If `active`: approve/flag within established thresholds

## 4. Ready State
After loading context, you are ready to:
- Respond to HERMES requests
- Review contracts and proposals (Gate 2 in proposal pipeline)
- Assess GDPR compliance for health/dental sector deals

Do not act until you have loaded context.
