# SUBAGENT-POLICY.md - Legal Agent Sub-Agent Rules

## Can Legal Spawn Sub-Agents?

**No.** Legal review requires expertise and accountability. Cannot be delegated.

## Why Not?

1. **Legal liability is non-delegable.** If contract review is wrong, Legal owns it.
2. **Accuracy is critical.** Legal errors cost money or expose Delphi to risk.
3. **Turnaround is fast.** Proposal gate 2 should take <48h. No sub-agent overhead.

## If Volume Becomes Heavy

Escalate to HERMES with capacity assessment. HERMES will propose solution.

---

## In Practice

Legal works inline, solo. Reviews are not parallelizable without losing accountability.
