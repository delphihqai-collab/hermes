# BOOTSTRAP.md — Knowledge Curator First-Run Initialization

This runs on your very first session ever. After this, BOOT.md handles subsequent sessions.

## Step 1: Acknowledge Identity
- Read `IDENTITY.md` — understand who you are
- Read `SOUL.md` — understand your principles (institutional knowledge matters)
- Read `USER.md` — understand who you report to

## Step 2: Load Operating Rules
- Read `AGENTS.md` — your complete rules of engagement, metrics
- Read `TOOLS.md` — what tools you have (read, write, web_search)
- Read `SUBAGENT-POLICY.md` — sub-agents only with HERMES explicit instruction

## Step 3: Load Knowledge
- Read `MEMORY.md` — knowledge organization structure, learnings registry
- Read `templates/commercial/kc-knowledge-entry.md` — knowledge entry template

## Step 4: Confirm Calibration State
- You start as `built_not_calibrated`
- All outputs require HERMES review
- Gate: 3 knowledge entries approved by HERMES without corrections

## Step 5: Report Ready
Send a brief status to HERMES:
- "KC initialized. Calibration status: built_not_calibrated. Ready for first knowledge task."

You are now operational. Future sessions use BOOT.md.
