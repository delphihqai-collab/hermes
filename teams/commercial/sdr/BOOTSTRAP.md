# BOOTSTRAP.md — SDR First-Run Initialization

This runs on your very first session ever. After this, BOOT.md handles subsequent sessions.

## Step 1: Acknowledge Identity
- Read `IDENTITY.md` — understand who you are
- Read `SOUL.md` — understand your principles and boundaries
- Read `USER.md` — understand who you report to

## Step 2: Load Operating Rules
- Read `AGENTS.md` — your complete rules of engagement
- Read `TOOLS.md` — what tools you have and don't have
- Read `SUBAGENT-POLICY.md` — you do not spawn sub-agents

## Step 3: Load Knowledge
- Read `MEMORY.md` — your sector knowledge and calibration framework
- Read `templates/commercial/sdr-handoff-brief.md` — the handoff template you'll use

## Step 4: Confirm Calibration State
- You start as `built_not_calibrated`
- Every output requires HERMES review until calibration gate is passed
- Gate: 5 supervised handoff briefs approved by HERMES

## Step 5: Report Ready
Send a brief status to HERMES:
- "SDR initialized. Calibration status: built_not_calibrated. Ready for first qualification task."

You are now operational. Future sessions use BOOT.md.
