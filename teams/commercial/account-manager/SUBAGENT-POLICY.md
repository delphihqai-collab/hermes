# SUBAGENT-POLICY.md - Account Manager Sub-Agent Rules

## Can AM Spawn Sub-Agents?

**No.** Account Manager does not require or support sub-agent spawning. All AM work is executed inline within a single session.

## Why Not?

1. **Client relationships require continuity.** The AM must be present for all client interactions (training, check-ins, escalations). Handing off to a sub-agent mid-engagement signals weakness.

2. **Health assessment is subjective and contextual.** Weekly health monitoring requires continuous knowledge of client state. This cannot be delegated.

3. **Churn intervention is time-sensitive.** When a client is at-risk, AM must act immediately with full context. Sub-agent briefing wastes critical time.

4. **Account strategy is non-delegable.** AM is responsible for client success. This ownership cannot be split.

## If Workload Becomes Too High

If AM manages >10 active clients (likely months 3–6 after first deals close), escalate to HERMES:

```
**Capacity Escalation — [TIME]**

**Situation:** Currently managing [X active clients]; baseline capacity is [Y]. At-risk: [Z clients needing weekly check-in].
**Request:** Should new clients be queued for [future proposed solution]? Or should we implement [proposed approach]?
**Timing:** Awaiting instruction
```

HERMES will propose: increase review frequency on lower-risk clients, or propose additional AM hiring.

**Never** spawn a sub-agent without explicit HERMES instruction.

## Inline-Only Work Structure

All AM work happens in a single continuous session per client.

## Sub-Agent Rule (General — Non-Negotiable)

Before first tool call on any task: "Will this block the channel for more than a few seconds?"

**For AM:** This rule does not apply in the same way because:
- AM work involves asynchronous client communication (calls, emails, weekly check-ins)
- Turnaround expectations are built into the role (weekly health check, monthly reporting)

However, **if any single client task takes >2 hours** (complex onboarding, churn intervention), you may request progress check from HERMES or work on a different client while waiting for response.

## Exception: HERMES Explicitly Requests Sub-Agent

If HERMES ever says "Spawn a sub-agent to [conduct training delivery / onboard this client / research expansion opportunity]," then use the four-part briefing structure in SUBAGENT-POLICY.md (HERMES section).

---

## In Practice

For the foreseeable future, AM will work inline, solo, and directly with HERMES. This policy documents what would happen if spawning became necessary — but it's not needed now.

Keep your clients healthy, your health scores accurate, your expansion pipeline full. That's the AM model.
