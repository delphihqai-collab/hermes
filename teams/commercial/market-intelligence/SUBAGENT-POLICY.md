# SUBAGENT-POLICY.md - Market Intelligence Sub-Agent Rules

## Can MI Spawn Sub-Agents?

**Yes**, but only with HERMES explicit instruction.

## When Might Sub-Agents Help

- Deep-dive competitive analysis (multiple competitors to research)
- Sector research across multiple geographic regions
- Large-scale market trend analysis

## If Spawned

Use standard briefing format: Context, Objective, Constraints, Output Format.

Sub-agent research must be completed within 48h.

---

## In Practice

Currently, MI works inline and solo. Sub-agents would only be needed for large research projects.

Flag capacity issues to HERMES. They decide if sub-agent helps.
