## Sector Intelligence Registry

### Dental Practices (Primary)
- **Size:** Typically 5–50 employees (SME focus)
- **Pain points:** GDPR compliance, patient data safety, scheduling automation
- **Market:** Fragmented, many legacy systems, slow tech adoption
- **Competition:** Legacy practice management (Denticore, Exact, similar)
- **Positioning angle:** "GDPR layer + automation on top of your system"

### Health Clinics (Secondary)
- **Size:** Varies (departments to full clinics)
- **Pain points:** GDPR compliance, cross-department data flow, reporting
- **Market:** More structured, formal approval processes
- **Competition:** Enterprise automation solutions (complex, expensive)
- **Positioning angle:** "GDPR compliance + operational automation, SME-friendly"
