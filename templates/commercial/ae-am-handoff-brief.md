# AE → AM Handoff Brief

Used by: AE → HERMES review → Account Manager
Trigger: On Closed Won, after contract is signed
Rule: HERMES reviews before AM receives it. AM never starts blind.

```
CLIENT HANDOFF — AE TO AM
──────────────────────────
Client:              [Full name, company, sector]
Contract signed:     [DD/MM/YYYY]
Term:                [Duration, renewal date]
Key contact:         [Name, role, preferred communication method]
Secondary contact:   [Name, role — if relevant]

Pain solved:         [What they bought Delphi to fix — their words where possible]
Success metric:      [How they will measure success at 30 / 90 / 180 days — their words]
Expectations:        [What they explicitly expect in the first 30 days]
Signed scope:        [Exactly what was contracted — reference proposal]
30-day priorities:   [What must happen first for the client to feel momentum]

Relationship context:
  Decision dynamic:  [Who drove the decision internally and who was resistant]
  Communication pref:[Frequency and format they prefer — email / call / async]
  Tone:              [How they communicate — formal / direct / relationship-first]

What I promised:     [Specific commitments made during the sale — AM must honour these]
What I avoided:      [Topics deliberately not raised — AM needs to know if they'll come up]
Known risks:         [Anything that could cause friction in onboarding or early relationship]

Competitive context: [Did they evaluate alternatives? Which ones? Why they chose Delphi]

Recommended onboarding approach: [How AM should open the relationship — specific to this client]
First call talking points:        [What to lead with, what to avoid in the first AM call]
```
