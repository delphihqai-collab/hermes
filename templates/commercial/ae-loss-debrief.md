# AE Loss Debrief

Used by: AE → HERMES → Knowledge Curator
Trigger: Mandatory on every Closed Lost before the Airtable record is closed
Rule: No deal is marked Lost without this filed first.

```
LOSS DEBRIEF
─────────────
Deal:                [Client name, company, sector]
Stage lost at:       [Which pipeline stage — Initial Contact / Demo / Needs Analysis / Proposal / Negotiation]
Date lost:           [DD/MM/YYYY]
Deal age:            [Days from first meeting to loss]

Primary reason:      [One sentence — the real reason, not the polite one]
Secondary reason:    [If applicable]
Competitor chosen:   [Name if known / Unknown / No competitor — chose status quo]
Price factor:        [Was pricing a primary objection? Y/N — detail the specific concern]

What we could have done differently:
  Targeting:         [Was this the right ICP? Right decision maker? Right timing?]
  Discovery:         [Any information we missed or misread about their situation]
  Demo:              [Any positioning gap or capability we couldn't demonstrate]
  Proposal:          [Any scope, pricing, timeline, or terms issue]
  Negotiation:       [Any concession handling or objection response that fell short]
  Process:           [Any gate delay, slow response, or internal friction that hurt us]

Client's stated reason:  [Exactly what they told us — verbatim if possible]
AE's assessed reason:    [What we actually believe happened]
Gap between the two:     [If there is one, why — what weren't they telling us?]

Re-engage potential:
  Verdict:           [Yes / No / Maybe]
  Trigger:           [What would need to change for re-engagement to make sense]
  Re-engage date:    [DD/MM/YYYY if Yes or Maybe / N/A if No]

Lessons for team:    [One or two things that should update our ICP, messaging, or process]
```
