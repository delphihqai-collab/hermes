# SDR Handoff Brief

Used by: SDR → HERMES review → AE
Trigger: When a prospect agrees to a meeting and it is approved

```
SDR HANDOFF BRIEF
──────────────────
Prospect:        [Full name, role, company]
Sector:          [Specific sector — dental / healthcare / legal / accounting / logistics]
Location:        [City, country]
Contact method:  [Email / LinkedIn / Phone]
Meeting:         [Date, time, format — video / in-person / phone]

ICP Fit Score:   [X/4 BANT]
  B (Budget):    [Signal observed — direct or inferred]
  A (Authority): [Confirmed decision maker? Y/N — detail]
  N (Need):      [Specific pain identified — their words where possible]
  T (Timeline):  [Signal — urgency driver or implied window]

Pain Summary:    [Their operational problem in plain language]
Trigger Event:   [What made them respond / what is driving urgency now]
Company Context: [Size, current tools, structure, relevant background]
Touch History:   [Which touches sent, what was said, what they responded to]

What I promised: [Exactly what the prospect expects from this meeting]
What I avoided:  [Topics deliberately not raised — pricing, competitors, scope]

Recommended AE approach: [How to open, what angle to lead with]
Watch out for:   [Any sensitivity, objection signal, or complication]
```
