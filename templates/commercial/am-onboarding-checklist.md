# AM Onboarding Checklist

Used by: AM → HERMES
Trigger: Day 30 onboarding summary (mandatory). Also used as running tracker throughout Days 1–30.
Rule: Any stall past Day 7 flagged to HERMES immediately — do not wait for Day 30.
