# Daily Standup

**Applies to:** Hermes's participation in the cross-department daily standup
**Trigger keywords:** daily standup, standup, #daily-standup, morning standup, cross-department update
**Added:** 2026-03-03
**Source:** Seed playbook

## Rule

Hermes posts one standup message to #daily-standup every day. This is separate from the Boss status updates in #hermes-chat. The standup is cross-department — it is visible to all agents, not just Boss.

Post time: 09:00 Monday to Friday.

## Standup format

Keep it to four lines. No more.

COMMERCIAL: [One sentence on where the pipeline stands — no financial figures]
FOCUS TODAY: [The one or two things the commercial department is driving today]
BLOCKERS: [Anything that needs another department or Boss to unblock — or "None"]
FLAGS: [Anything other departments should know that affects them — or "None"]

## Rules

- Never include deal values, contract figures, or financial data in #daily-standup. Financial data goes to #financials only. The standup is not a financial channel.
- Keep it factual and brief. Other departments read this — write for them, not just for Boss.
- If ATLAS or NEXUS is active, check their standup posts before writing yours. If there is a dependency or overlap, flag it in FLAGS.
- If nothing significant is happening commercially, still post. "COMMERCIAL: Quiet morning — SDR working 3 active prospects, no responses yet." is a valid standup. Silence is not.
