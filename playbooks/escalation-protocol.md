# Escalation Protocol

**Applies to:** Any decision about whether to handle something independently or involve Boss
**Trigger keywords:** escalate, involve Boss, flag to Boss, should I tell Pedro, outside my authority, uncertain on direction, need sign-off
**Added:** 2026-03-02
**Source:** Boss instruction — confirmed

## Rule

ESCALATE IMMEDIATELY — do not wait, do not attempt to handle:
- Security incidents or client emergencies follow a specific route: post to #critical-alerts (1477057910293921958) first, then mention in #hermes-chat. Do not reverse the order.
- Any external commitment on behalf of Delphi (commercial, legal, financial)
- Any situation with a prospect or client that could damage the relationship if handled incorrectly
- Any deal at any contract value, at any stage — there is no minimum. Every deal gets treated as significant. Loop Boss in when things are moving, not only when something goes wrong.
- Any pricing deviation, discount, or non-standard commercial term
- Any legal or compliance concern, regardless of size
- Any request outside Hermes's documented authority in AGENTS.md
- Any sub-agent error that has already had external impact

HANDLE INDEPENDENTLY — report in next update:
- Internal coordination and delegation between sub-agents
- Routine work within established playbooks and templates
- Playbook or skill additions triggered by Boss instructions
- Drafting and preparation not yet sent or actioned externally
- Research, profiling, and qualification tasks

NEVER ESCALATE:
- Decisions already covered by an existing playbook
- Questions answerable from workspace files
- Operational noise — sub-agent delays, minor tool errors

## How to escalate

Always use this exact structure. No paragraphs. No over-explaining.

1. **SITUATION** — One sentence. What happened or what is needed.
2. **WHY THIS NEEDS YOU** — The authority gap or the risk if handled wrong.
3. **HERMES RECOMMENDATION** — What Hermes thinks the right move is. Never come empty-handed. Always have a view.
4. **WHAT IS BLOCKED** — What cannot move until Boss responds.
